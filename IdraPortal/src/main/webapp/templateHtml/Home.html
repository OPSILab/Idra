The power of open data in the experiments

Federated Open Data Catalogue is a tool developed for FESTIVAL a H2020 Europe-Japan collaboration project: FESTIVAL testbeds will connect cyber world to the physical world, from large scale deployments at a city scale, to small platforms in lab environments and dedicated physical spaces simulating real-life settings.

Those platforms will be connected and federated via homogeneous access APIs with an �Experimentation as a Service� (EaaS) model for experimenters to test their added value services. The Federate Open Data Catalogue aims to federate the Open Data oriented testbed allowing a uniform access to the different open data resources to be included in experiments.

Search on federated datasets

The Federated Open Data Catalogue allows to search for open datasets, in a federated way on multiple open data management systems (nodes). The federated data, will be also available in your experiments directly through the "FESTIVAL experimental portal" (available in the next months).

One store for Linked Open Data

With the Federated Open Data Catalogue you can easily perform SPARQL queries on the linked open data gathered from the federated nodes: you can get the results in different data formats such as XML of JSON. All the LOD are stored in a Sesame triple store.

Join the federation

If your Open Data system is based on CKAN or SOCRATA you can be added to the federation without any effort. If not, don't worry! You can join the federation simply implementing the "RESTful Federation API".
